SHORE : THE ROLLING OF THE TIDES

Shore attempts to replicate the behaviors of rolling tides washing upon shore. The audience is not solely an observer of this natural phenomenon but also a participant who can interfere with the waves and the sand. It is a stimulation that seeks to magnify humanity's role as an interference of nature.

Press on the sand to take a step in the sand.

Press on the water to take a step into the crashing waves.

Press on 'r' to speed up the tides

Press on 's' to speed up the tides
